Centrality Bar Charts

These two bar charts show the centrality of all 130 champions. Centrality is the sum of all of the affinity values for that champion to every other champion. The higher the value, the more likely that champion is closely related to an arbitrary champion.

Insights

  • Lucian, Lee Sin, and Ezreal score well here again.
  • There's exponential decay at the top of the chart. Strong dropoff for the first 10 champions or so, then a more linear decrease.
  • Interestingly, a lot of the champions at the bottom of the chart have been talked about in terms of reworks...
  • Alas, poor Yorick.

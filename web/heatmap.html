Affinity Heatmap

Our heatmap shows how closely related champions are to one another in our dataset. The darker the cell, the more related the two champions are. You can see the specific affinity percentage by mousing over a cell. Clicking a champion's name on one of the axes will sort the chart by that champion.

Insights

  • We can quickly identify the top affinity for each champion by sorting by that champion.
  • The top champions next to your favorite after sorting (besides themselves) might be good choices for your next champion!
  • Challenger/Master is pretty top heavy, with about 20-25% champions having strong affinity with one another. This confirms our intuition that the meta is more strongly enforced at the highest skill levels.
  • Sorting the chart by two of our most centralized champions (Lucian/Ezreal) gives us a good approximation of champion popularity.
  • Future iterations of this metric could do a better job differentiating between less popular champions. We think this is also an artifact of our chosen sample.

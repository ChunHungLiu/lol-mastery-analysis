Force Graph (Top Affinity)

The links on this graph represent the top affinity relationship for each champion; each champion was allocated one link. Left clicking a champion will bring up a link to their Mobafire page. Try dragging around the nodes in the chart to understand how they "move" together! Champions highlighted in red have been identified as part of the "meta" cluster: for more information on how the meta was determined, see the about page.

Insights

  • Lucian/Ezreal, Lee Sin, and Thresh are the "focal points" of the meta.
  • We see some interesting clustering behavior on this graph:
    • Lucian/Ezreal have a lot of ADC/AP champions with some easy to play champs from the bruiser/tank/melee carry category.
    • Lee Sin has a lot of the bruiser/tank/melee carry champions with some easy to play champs from the AP/ADC category.
    • The Thresh cluster might be the most interesting; everyone in his sub-graph has been mostly played as support since the mastery system was released.
  • Our meta clustering does a pretty good job, by our intuition, of identifying the important champions in LoL since the release of the mastery system. Champions reworked or released since the mastery update were unfairly penalized, however (Poppy being a notable example)

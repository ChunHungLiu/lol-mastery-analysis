Radial Chart

Another way of exploring champion affinity; any affinity above 15% between champions is represented on this chart. If a champion didn't have any affinities over 15%, we chose the top affinity for that champion (so nobody was left out). You can mouseover a champion's label to have that champion's links "pop" from the graph.

Radial charts are a great example of data as art. Data visualizations like this give us the warm fuzzies we imagine artists get. :)

Insights

  • The "thicker" a specific champion's initial set of edges are, the more interconnected they are (this gives some hints towards the results of the centrality metric).
  • It's clear from the chart how top-heavy the meta is. A good chunk of champions only have a single link.
